How this works
Writing
  • > starts a task
  • Indent (Tab) under a task for notes
  • End a line with : to make a section header
Organizing
  • Drag a task's left grip right to complete it
  • Drag a task up / down to reorder it
  • Drag a task onto a tab to move it there
  • Click a section band to collapse / expand it
  • Click the corner triangle to reset a task's age
Lists & team
  • Tabs: click to switch · double-click to rename · + for a new list
  • Team shows everyone's lists at once
  • ⤢ opens one person's list full-screen
  • ✎ lets you add a task to someone's Assigned column — it never touches their real list
Other
  • Assigned column: tasks others added for you; + moves one onto your list, × dismisses it
  • A red ! on the collapsed Assigned tab means something new is waiting
  • Completed panel: click "restore" to bring a task back
  • export .txt downloads your list as plain text
  • Your list follows your name — type it again on a new computer to get it back
  • Sign out clears your name from this device
